Unlike traditional internships, our Budapest Vanguard Cohort is designed to provide deep industry immersion in a supportive environment with like-minded peers.

Industry Connections

Morning: Project Work

Start your day at the tech hub, collaborating with your team on client projects. Work alongside industry mentors who provide guidance and feedback on your work.

Attend specialized workshops led by industry experts on cutting-edge technologies and methodologies used in European tech companies.

Evening: Networking & Culture

Join tech meetups, networking events, or cultural experiences around Budapest. Build connections while experiencing the city's unique blend of history and innovation.
