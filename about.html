Our Story

Skymirror Academy began in 2019 when a group of industry veterans became frustrated with the gap between traditional education and what the tech industry actually needed. They saw new graduates struggling to apply theoretical knowledge to practical challenges.

Our founders—a mix of experienced developers, designers, and entrepreneurs—came together to create a different kind of tech education: one that emphasizes real-world projects, practical skills, and meaningful connections to industry.

The Budapest Vanguard Cohort program emerged from our belief that immersive experiences accelerate learning and career growth. By placing students in an international tech hub, working on actual projects with real companies, we provide an educational experience that can't be replicated in a classroom.

Today, Skymirror Academy has helped hundreds of students transform their careers, with many going on to work at leading tech companies or starting their own ventures. We remain committed to our original mission: making tech education practical, immersive, and connected to real-world opportunities.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at Skymirror Academy, from curriculum design to student support.

Learn By Doing

We believe that practical application trumps theory every time. Our programs are designed around hands-on projects that mirror real-world challenges in the tech industry.

Community First

Learning happens best in supportive communities. We foster a collaborative environment where students learn from each other as much as from their instructors.

Industry Relevance

Our curriculum is constantly updated to reflect current industry needs and emerging technologies, ensuring students graduate with immediately applicable skills.

Global Perspective

Technology is borderless. We prepare students for international careers through exposure to global tech trends and multicultural team experiences.

World‑Class Mentorship
