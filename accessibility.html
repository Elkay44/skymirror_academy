Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making education accessible to everyone

Last updated: January 2025

Our Commitment

Skymirror Academy is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to achieve these goals.

We believe that technology education should be accessible to all, regardless of ability, and we strive to create an inclusive learning environment that empowers every student to succeed.

Accessibility Standards

Our website aims to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at the AA level. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and user-friendly for everyone.

We also follow accessibility best practices including:

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen readers
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • High contrast color schemes
  • Alternative text for images
  • Descriptive link text and headings
  • Focus indicators for interactive elements

Accessibility Features

Our website and educational platform include the following accessibility features:

Navigation

  • Skip to main content link
  • Consistent navigation structure across all pages
  • Breadcrumb navigation where applicable
  • Logical tab order for keyboard users

Visual Design

  • High contrast color combinations
  • Scalable text that can be enlarged up to 200%
  • Support for high contrast mode
  • Reduced motion options for users with vestibular disorders

Content

  • Descriptive headings and labels
  • Alternative text for all meaningful images
  • Captions and transcripts for video content
  • Clear and simple language

Forms

  • Clearly labeled form fields
  • Error messages that are easy to understand
  • Required field indicators
  • Keyboard accessible form controls

Educational Accessibility

Beyond our website, we are committed to making our educational programs accessible:

Learning Materials

  • Multiple format options (text, audio, video)
  • Closed captions for all video content
  • Screen reader compatible documents
  • Adjustable playback speeds for video content

Learning Support

  • Extended time for assignments when needed
  • Alternative assessment methods
  • One-on-one mentoring support
  • Flexible scheduling options

Technology Tools

  • Screen reader compatible development environments
  • Voice recognition software support
  • Keyboard-only navigation for all tools
  • High contrast themes for coding interfaces

Assistive Technologies

Our website and learning platform are designed to work with assistive technologies, including:

  • Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver, TalkBack)
  • Voice recognition software (Dragon NaturallySpeaking)
  • Keyboard navigation tools
  • Browser zoom and magnification tools
  • High contrast and dark mode extensions

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, we acknowledge some current limitations:

  • Some third-party embedded content may not be fully accessible
  • Complex interactive coding exercises may require additional support
  • Some legacy video content may lack captions (being updated)

We are actively working to address these limitations and welcome feedback on how we can improve.

Accommodation Requests

If you need specific accommodations to participate in our programs, please contact us. We will work with you to provide:

  • Alternative formats for course materials
  • Modified assessment methods
  • Extended deadlines when appropriate
  • Additional technical support
  • Personalized learning plans

Feedback and Contact

  • The assistive technology you're using
  • The browser and operating system
  • A description of the problem encountered

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing commitment. Our efforts include:

  • Regular accessibility audits and testing
  • Staff training on accessibility best practices
  • User testing with people with disabilities
  • Continuous improvement based on feedback
  • Staying current with accessibility standards and technologies

Third-Party Content

Some content on our website may be provided by third parties. While we strive to ensure all content meets accessibility standards, we cannot guarantee the accessibility of third-party content. If you encounter issues with third-party content, please contact us and we will work to find alternative solutions.
