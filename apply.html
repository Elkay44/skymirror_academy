Student Scholarship (30% Discount)

We offer a 30% tuition discount specifically for current university/college students. You may select only one scholarship category per application.

Student Scholarship Criteria (30% Discount):

  • • Enrollment Verification: Currently enrolled in university/college
  • • Academic Performance: Good academic standing (GPA 3.0+ preferred)
  • • Career Alignment: Clear connection between studies and tech career goals
  • • Financial Need: Demonstrated need for tuition assistance (if applicable)
  • • Commitment: Dedication to completing the program successfully
  • • Application Quality: Thoughtful, detailed responses
